ABOUT AGM

AGM is an independent group of professional service provider firms based in Belgrade, Serbia, established and present in the market from 2013. Since then, AGM has been recognized as a high-level boutique advisory firm, supporting the multinational companies in their operations in the region and world-wide on a continuous, custom-fit basis.

AGM is, directly and throughout our associates, covering jurisdictions of Serbia, Montenegro and Bosnia and Herzegovina, while our international outlook and client base is spread world-wide.

AGM team is composed of profound experts in the areas of financial and accounting advisory, audit and assurance, international taxation and transfer pricing, as well as legal advisory. Our key experts have generated extensive knowledge in their respective fields of expertise by working with some of the biggest multinational clients on arguably the most complex types of engagements for more than a decade. Enheartened by the positive feedback from our clients, we are keeping the pace with the latest global developments and expanding the areas of our expertise beyond the traditional service lines.

The key factor behind the success of AGM is our approach: we involve our clients in every step of our work, starting from the fact finding, to the implementation of our client-tailored advice. By doing this, we significantly share and transfer valuable knowledge and at the same time make sure no detail is missed. No challenge is too big for us. Being of entrepreneurial spirit, we are focused on providing the “added value” to our clients from each service line we furnish. In simple words, we seek to delight our clients.

AGM started its international affiliation with Mazars group in 2018, by agreeing on the exclusive correspondent status for Serbia. With this, AGM brought to Serbia one of the world’s biggest integrated international organizations, specializing in audit, accountancy, tax, legal and advisory services. By agreeing with Mazars, AGM also became a correspondent firm of Praxity, one of the world’s biggest alliance of independent accountancy and consulting firms. Synergy with Mazars marks for AGM start of a new phase in its development, whereas AGM aims high to establish itself as a benchmark in respect of the quality for the rest of the market.
